Cultivar is a Minecraft plugin that adds realistic crop cultivation mechanics. Grow Cannabis, Tobacco, Tea, and Mushrooms with unique growth stages, care requirements, and post-harvest processing.

## Features

- **Four Crop Types** - Each with distinct growth mechanics and requirements
- **Strain Genetics** - Procedurally generated strains with unique properties
- **Post-Harvest Processing** - Drying, curing, fermentation, and brewing
- **Reputation System** - Four ranks tracking your cultivation skill
- **Smoking Pipes** - Three tiers with consumable effects

## Requirements

- Minecraft 1.20.1
- Paper or Spigot server
- Java 17+
