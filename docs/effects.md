## Overview

Items in Cultivar provide various effects when consumed (smoking or drinking). The potency and duration are influenced by the item quality, strain genetics, and processing method.

## Smoking Effects

The following are the default effects when smoking with a pipe or using rolled items.

### Cannabis

**Cannabis Bud**
- **Slowness II** (40s)
- **Hunger I** (20s)
- **Night Vision** (60s)
- **Nausea** (3s)

**Fermented Bud**
- All effects have **+50% duration**.
- Potency tier increases by 1.

### Tobacco

**Tobacco Leaf**
- **Speed I** (30s)
- **Saturation I** (10s)

**Cigarette**
- Same as Tobacco Leaf but with easier usage.

### Combined Items

**Spliff**
- Combines the effects of both Cannabis and Tobacco.
- Each effect duration is multiplied by **0.7x** for balance.

**Herbal Fill**
- **Night Vision** (120s)
- Special earthy aroma.

## Tea Effects

Drinking tea provides a base set of buffs depending on the variety.

### Base Varieties

| Tea | Effects | Message |
|-----|---------|---------|
| **Green Tea** | Regeneration I (15s), Saturation I (10s) | "Refreshing" |
| **Black Tea** | Speed I (30s), Haste I (20s) | "Bold and rich" |
| **Herbal Tea** | Night Vision (60s), Slow Falling I (30s) | "Earthy and calm" |

### Tea Blends

Adding specific ingredients to the off-hand during brewing adds extra properties:

- **Sweet Berries**: Regeneration I (5s).
- **Sugar**: Removes bitterness (purely flavor/smoothness).
- **Smoky Chai**: Speed I (10s).

### Quality Multipliers

- **Weak**: 0.5x duration.
- **Perfect**: 1.5x duration.
- **Bitter**: Adds **Nausea** (5s) but increases effect **Amplifier** by 1.

## Processing Benefits

Processing items through curing or fermentation significantly improves their value.

### Cured Tobacco Benefits

| Cure Type | Primary Benefit |
|-----------|-----------------|
| **Light-cured** | Standard smooth experience. |
| **Dark-cured** | Enhanced effect duration. |
| **Fire-cured** | Adds +20% Saturation effectiveness. |
| **Air-cured** | Slow-dried, provides the smoothest base effects. |
| **Aged** | All effects improved by 10%. |

### Fermentation Benefits

- **Fermented Bud**: Guaranteed higher potency tier than the raw harvest.
- **Aged Tobacco**: Deepened flavor and extended buff duration.

## Genetic Influence

Every strain has a unique profile that modifies these base effects. Check your [Strain Journal](mechanics#strain-genetics) to see how your specific harvest performs.
